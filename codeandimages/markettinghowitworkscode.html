How PawTaker Works

Join our trusted community of pet lovers. We've simplified pet care through a points-based exchange system that rewards your love for animals.

verified_user
STEP 01

Sign up & Verify (KYC)

Create your personal profile and complete our secure identity check. We prioritize safety by ensuring every member of our community is verified through a standard KYC process.

payments
STEP 02

Earn Points by Sitting

Ready to hang out with some furry friends? Browse local requests and offer your pet-sitting services. For every hour you spend caring for a pet, you accumulate "PawPoints" in your wallet.

volunteer_activism
STEP 03

Spend Points for Care

Planning a trip or need a helping hand? Use your earned PawPoints to book trusted sitters from the community. It's a cashless exchange built on mutual support and a shared love for pets.

grade
STEP 04

Rate & Review

After every sitting session, share your experience. High ratings help members build trust, ensuring the PawTaker community remains a safe and high-quality environment for all our pets.
